About Space X

Have you ever wantyed to go to space in style?

SpaceX was founded on the belief that humans should be a multiplanetary species. The mission of SpaceX is to see this goal through by creating safe, cost efficient, reusable rockets to take people to the ISS, the Moon, and beyond. SpaceX has gained attention worldwide for their historic milestone of becoming the first private company to take humans to the ISS. They also offer commercial flights for private passengers to both Earth and the Lunar orbit.
